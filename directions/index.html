Directions

University of Southern California
Michaelson Building, 1002 Childs Way, Los Angeles CA, 90089.

Public transit:
See USC's page for details , including the map of metro connections.

Driving directions:
To access the USC campus and the parking garage, enter campus at the gate located at Jefferson Blvd and McClintock Avenue. Stop at the small booth on the left to request a parking pass, indicating you are attending the SoCal SysBio meeting (Parking is included with your registration). Proceed to the Downey Way structure (straight ahead on the right, at the corner of McClintock Ave and Downey Way).
